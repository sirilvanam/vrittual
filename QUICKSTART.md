## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit entries after adding them?**
A: Currently you can delete and re-add. Edit functionality is planned.

**Q: Does this sync across devices?**
A: Yes, if you enable iCloud for the app, your data syncs automatically.

**Q: Can I track macros (protein, carbs, fats)?**
A: Not yet, but it's on the roadmap!

**Q: What if I eat half a serving?**
A: For now, you'll need to calculate it yourself. Future versions will support custom serving sizes.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Not currently, but it's a planned feature.
