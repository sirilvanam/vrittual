### Key Visual Elements:
- **Navigation Bar**: White background, "Calorie Tracker" title centered
- **Settings Icon**: Gray gear icon, top-left
- **Add Button**: Blue plus icon, top-right, large and prominent
- **Date Picker**: Compact style, gray background card, rounded corners
- **Progress Circle**: 
  - Large (220x220 points)
  - 20pt stroke width
  - Green when under goal, red when over
  - Smooth animation when updating
  - Gray background ring (20% opacity)
- **Calorie Number**: 48pt bold font, black
- **Stats Section**: Two columns with divider
- **Entry Cards**: Light gray background, 8pt corner radius, 16pt padding
- **Meal Headers**: Semi-bold, secondary gray color
- **Delete Icons**: Red trash can, right side of each entry
